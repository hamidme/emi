About Me

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Chemical Sciences at Fountain University, Nigeria, and a PhD-trained Polymer Chemist with international academic and professional experience across Nigeria, Malaysia, Oman, and the United Arab Emirates.

My current work bridges Chemistry, Artificial Intelligence, and Information Technology through applications of cheminformatics, QSAR modelling, computational toxicology, and machine learning. I am currently pursuing an MSc in Information Technology (Cheminformatics) at the International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM).

I am also the Founder of the African Cheminformatics Initiative (ACI), a capacity-building platform dedicated to advancing cheminformatics education and research across Africa.

Current Projects

Phthalate Contaminants in Nigerian Plastic Waste

Cheminformatics profiling and computational toxicology assessment of phthalate compounds associated with Nigerian plastic waste streams.

African Cheminformatics Initiative (ACI)

A capacity-building platform advancing cheminformatics education, training, and collaborative research across Africa.

Our mission

ACI aims to strengthen research capacity in cheminformatics by connecting early-career researchers, educators, and interdisciplinary collaborators across the continent.

Focus areas

  • Mentorship for African researchers in computational chemistry
  • Workshops and collaborative problem-solving initiatives
